HomeKit Menu

Control your HomeKit devices from the Mac menu bar

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 14.0 or later

Features

Menu Bar Access

Quick access to all your HomeKit devices right from the menu bar. Toggle lights, switches, and more with a single click.

Apple Shortcuts

Run your favorite Apple Shortcuts directly from the menu. Organize by folders and access your automations instantly.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Assign custom keyboard shortcuts to any device. Control your home without touching the mouse.

Sensors & Scenes

View sensor readings like temperature and humidity. Trigger HomeKit scenes with one click.

Room Organization

Devices automatically grouped by room. Select exactly which devices appear in your menu.

Auto Updates

Built-in update checker keeps you on the latest version with in-app downloads.

Matches Your Mac

Automatically adapts to your system appearance
