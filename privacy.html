Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

HomeKit Menu does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data We Collect

None. HomeKit Menu does not collect any data from you or your devices.

How the App Works

HomeKit Menu connects directly to your HomeKit devices through Apple's HomeKit framework. All communication happens locally between your Mac and your HomeKit home hub (Apple TV, HomePod, or iPad).

  • Your device data stays on your devices
  • No data is sent to Generous Corp or any third party
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No account required

Network Connections

HomeKit Menu only makes network connections for:

  • HomeKit communication: Local network connections to your HomeKit hub, handled entirely by Apple's frameworks
  • Update checks: When you click "Check for Updates," the app connects to our GitHub repository to check for new versions. No personal information is transmitted.

Data Storage

Your preferences (which devices to show, display settings, keyboard shortcuts) are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

HomeKit Menu uses:

  • Apple HomeKit: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub: For update checks and downloads, subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
